Turning left over Mont Blanc (MOBLO) routing to Roma FCO . When flying in twilight and night is about to fall you can see "night" approaching from the East and it looks like a darkish line which starts at the horizon and as time progresses the line gets bigger and bigger until it passes overhead. You are now flying in the dark. It's a very impressive sight and one you can only see from high up in the sky.
