Many of you have asked to know more about our CEO, Mrs. Erica Gwyn, a genuine and down to earth "Nonprofit Guru'. Drawing on more than 20 years of professional experiences in philanthropy, Erica has built the foundation for some of Atlanta's greatest organizations including key fundraising and/or leadership roles with Atlanta Habitat for Humanity,  Americorps, Spelman College, Girls Inc. Of Greater Atlanta, Fulton County Schools and the Partnership Against Domestic Violence. Drawing on both her personal and professional skills has empowered her with the focus, spirit and tenacity to assist organizations at various stages of fundraising. Erica is a proud graduate of The University of Georgia (BS Education) and has a Master's degree in Health Administration from Central Michigan University.
