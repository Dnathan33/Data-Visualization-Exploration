A special exhibition of China’s Qin (221–206 B.C.) and Han (206 B.C.-220 A.D.) dynasty relics will open from April 3 at New York's Metropolitan Museum of Art, China.com.cn reported on March 27. The exhibition of Chinese culture, including 164 items, will be the largest of its kind this year in the U.S. It will highlight art and culture from the Qin and Han dynasties across more than four centuries, showcasing archaeological discoveries and historical research carried out over the past 50 years. #nyc #newyork #chinese #relics #exhibition #culture #qindynasty #handynasty #metropolitanmuseum #museum
