Tasty, delicious and super healthy! This place is one of my favourite new finds in New York. With a few locations dotted around the city I would thoroughly recommend a visit if you like smoothies and just healthy foods that are fresh and give you that feel good factor! My favourite is this coffee-n-cacao smoothie, bloody amazing! With music that makes you feel like your in the carribean (it did when I visited!) it gives you a great start to your day.
