Queen Latifah ‎– Black Reign (Motown, 1993) "U.N.I.T.Y." was the first single and spoke out against the disrespect of women in society and hiphop- culture. Latifah is  one of the founders of "The Flavor Unit", a crew of emcees and DJs from New York City and Northern New Jersey with such members as "Naughty By Nature" or "Black Sheep" and much more.
