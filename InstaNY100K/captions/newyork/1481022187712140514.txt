How incredible do these Golden Tumeric Apple Pancakes by @rrayyme look 😍😍 We can't wait to try her recipe!💛 * 1/2 cup fresh grated apple * 1 1/4 cup flour * 1/2 cup almond milk * 1/4 cup yogurt * 2 tbsp maple syrup * 1 tsp baking powder * 1 tsp vanilla extract * ½ tsp cinnamon * ½ tsp ground turmeric * ¼ tsp ginger * 1 tsp lemon juice Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth and pour 1/4 cup at a time into medium high heat pan and flip over once it bubbles. Cook both sides until golden brown. Serve with almond butter, pecans and more fresh sliced apples🍎👌🏼
