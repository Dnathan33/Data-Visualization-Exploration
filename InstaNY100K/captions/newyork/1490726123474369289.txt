What is a brand? More than a logo or product, a brand is a promise of quality and reputation. It’s knowing and consistently living from a true identity. Branding connects directly to consumer needs, emotions and competitive environments. For Country Carpet, the Didit team reduced it down to a single phrase: “Art for home.” Beyond innovative interior design, Country Carpet offers the superior selection to transform modern day living rooms into museums. Discover how Didit blurred the lines between history’s most exquisite art and today’s highest quality interior style.
