T R Y H A R D E R • • •  Feeling Jason Lazarus motivational campaign via 2008. • • • • "Try Harder" is not only the name of an Otis Redding record, but also a piece by Chicago based artist Jason Lazarus, showing an image of a determined Otis on his knees with "Try Harder" written across it. Jason installed the 9' x 12' motivational piece at Heaven Gallery in 2008 and has been distributing free small newsprint versions of the poster via SASE ever since. His website shows a submitted image gallery of places where people feel they needed the most motivation.
