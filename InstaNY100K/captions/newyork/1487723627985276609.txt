The U.S. premiere of “Simultaneous Duo Versions” - a collaboration between John Armleder and Christian Marclay - will be showed at The Kitchen on April 7 and 8 (ticket is required). The artists present seminal works by George Brecht, John Cage, Kurt Schwitters, and LaMonte Young, in addition to their own works from the 1960s onward. Organized by Tim Griffin and Lumi Tan.
