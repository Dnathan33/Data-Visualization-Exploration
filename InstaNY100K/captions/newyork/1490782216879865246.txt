Melodie Foellmi, Brookyln LINK Clinical Director, Edward Fernandez, Clinical Director at Queens TASC, and Lillian Bopp, Coordinator, attended the 23rd Annual @nationaltasc Conference in New Orleans!  Every year, the National TASC Conference brings together professionals from around the country to learn about the latest treatment advancements. National TASC's mission is to offer leadership, advocacy, and policy recommendations for innovative treatment and recovery supports resulting in opportunities for justice-involved individuals with behavioral health needs to achieve healthy and productive lives with their families and communities. #tasc #treatment #brooklyn #newyork #nyc #neworleans #longisland #ntascnola17
