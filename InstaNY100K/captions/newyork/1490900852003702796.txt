Meet Bryan Stevenson. . Bryan is an acclaimed public interest lawyer, social justice activist, founder and Executive Director of the @eji_org, and a clinical professor at the @nyuniversity School of Law.  Mr. Stevenson has dedicated his career to fighting poverty and challenging racial discrimination in the criminal justice system. He has won numerous awards including the prestigious MacArthur Foundation “Genius” Prize and the National Medal of Liberty from the @aclu_nationwide. . Bryan understands the importance of education and that underserved students in segregated schools are more likely to end up in the criminal justice system. Hear his thoughts in #TeachUsAll
