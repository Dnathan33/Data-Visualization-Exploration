Bathrooms are one of the most important rooms in a house but they tend to need frequent maintenance & renovations. Which is quite expensive so when redesigning your bathrooms you should consider investing in a timeless design.  Tips on how to create a timeless bathroom: • Color: go for White, Cream or Beige, it will always look fresh and clean. They are timeless colors that are always trendy and can easily be styled with colorful accessories to add a pop of color. • Materials: 1)Marble 2)Stone 3)Ceramics.
