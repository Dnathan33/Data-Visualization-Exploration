RELEASE DATE2017-04-07 LABELS @blackholerecordings CATALOG #BH8150 http://classic.beatport.com/release/had-to-leave-rhys-fulber-and-kris-oneil-mix/1982729  Russian born, US residing singer Anastasia Bykova, also know is Nastya, makes her debut on Black Hole Recordings with the atmospheric piece of electronica that is Had To Leave. The emotional vocals of Nastya is backed up by the soundscapes of the mastermind that is Rhys Fulber (also known as Conjure One and one half of platinum selling act Delerium) and Black Hole resident Kris O'Neil, making Had To Leave a must-have in the collection of any ambient music lover.
